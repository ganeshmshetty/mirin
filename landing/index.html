FAQ

Quick answers to the most common setup questions.

Does Mirin replace scrcpy?

No. Mirin is a GUI that makes scrcpy easier to use and configure.

Can I mirror wirelessly?

Yes. The wireless setup wizard walks you through TCP/IP mode.

Can I manage multiple devices?

Mirin tracks connected and saved devices, plus multiple active sessions.

Which OS versions are supported?

Windows 10 and 11, with Android 5.0 and newer devices.
